OUR STORY

OUR LEGACY

Our Legacy of Excellence

Founded in 1990, Siberian Pure has grown from a small family-owned dairy farm into one of Siberia’s most trusted milk producers.

Our milk is 100% organic, produced under the supervision of experts who ensure every process — from feeding to packaging — meets the highest quality standards.

We care deeply for our cows, conducting regular medical checkups to maintain their health and well-being. This care reflects in the taste and purity of our milk, proudly rated as Siberia’s No.1.

With over 2200 skilled workers and collaborations with numerous agencies, Siberian Pure operates major centers across Russia and Europe.

Today, our commitment to purity and excellence has led us to achieve over 1 crore+ in international business, spreading the authentic Siberian taste across the globe.
